THE CAST OF SONIC RE:MIX

SONIC THE HEDGEHOG

The blue blur filled with attitude, Sonic's always itching to embark on a new venture! With Eggman gone for vacation, he starts to feel like things are different with that new villain and the new evil gang called "The Krusty Kremlin". What he knows now is to stop them from stealing it and must return it to where it came from.

MILES "TAILS" PROWER

Sonic's closest ally and most trusted friend, this curious fox is always willing to follow alongside Sonic in his adventures! After scanning the badniks that disturbed their vacation, Tails (alongside Sonic) make haste for their source location, Kuroku!

METAL SONIC (MOON OF THE METAL)

The Robot from the moon facility was pacified and became a good guy by Tails with his Pacify_Robot.exe data drive. Metal Sonic seeks out for the moonlight and joins in the 5 Zatchitatos team. As long as they stick together, they will be alright.

AMY ROSE

The Delightful pink hedgehog girl with her Moon Hammer helps her slice and dice any foe that gets in her way and she likes Sonic a lot. She was a part of the 5 Zatchitatos team and they sticked together like birds of a feather.

RAY THE FLYING SQUIRREL

The Bright-sided yellow squirrel jumps into the adventure with Sonic, Tails, Metal Sonic & Amy as they work together to be a group. This flying squirrel capes into action and soars through the air from dangerous rickety-rallies. He escaped Eggman before. but he's going after Kuroku to many places while his pals Knuckles, Mighty, Fang & Tunnel be busy doing other things.
