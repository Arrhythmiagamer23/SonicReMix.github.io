Sonic the Hedgehog Re:Mix is a fan-made interpretation and a moon facility mod of the next level with the 5 heroes join together known as "The 5 Zatchitatos". This game is called "Prism Legend", based on and inspired by the game "Captain Toad: Prism Star Quest".

This game will surprise you with unexpected ways from the styles of the games of CORE FRAMEWORK, DKC4 Engine and More!

While authenticity to hardware limitations is considered, Sonic the Hedgehog Re:Mix draws from a number inspirations, not letting itself be limited to purely what systems of the time were capable of, nor what SEGA themselves would have necessarily done.

With new zones, new concepts, new characters, a new setting, and more, Re:Mix aims to put its own unique spin on the classic 2D Sonic formula, making a spinout of the gameplay after the Fallen Star and Moon Facility & Galactic Sonic Fangames.
